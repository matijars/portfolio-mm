About me

Hello everyone! My name is Matija, and I am a frontend developer. In 2019, I decided to change my career and start learning something that had always intrigued me: web development. Prior to this, I spent eight years working in various administrative roles. While these jobs provided me with valuable work experience, I always knew they weren't my true passion.


I began exploring various online tutorials and found a couple of YouTube channels focused on Front End Development that caught my attention. A friend who works as a senior Front End developer guided me as I created several projects, which are listed in my CV and in project section. This journey ignited my passion for learning new technologies.


I have accumulated professional experience from multiple front-end development roles. I began as a Front-End Developer at Ingsoftware, where I also completed a two-month internship program, and then continued as a Software Engineer at Gerniks. In both positions, I worked on a variety of projects, including internal initiatives and client-based projects, which helped broaden my skill set and knowledge base. During this time, I specialized in the Angular framework and contributed to the development of two major projects. I am currently working as a Frontend Developer at Horisen, where I continue to refine my expertise and contribute to innovative projects.
