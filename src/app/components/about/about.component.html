About me

Hello everyone! My name is Matija, and I am a frontend developer. In 2019, I decided to change my career and start learning something that had always intrigued me: web development. Prior to this, I spent eight years working in various administrative roles. While these jobs provided me with valuable work experience, I always knew they weren't my true passion.


I began exploring various online tutorials and found a couple of YouTube channels focused on Front End Development that caught my attention. A friend who works as a senior Front End developer guided me as I created several projects, which are listed in my CV and i project section. This journey ignited my passion for learning new technologies.


I have accumulated professional experience from two distinct roles: initially, as a Front-End Developer at Ingsoftware, where I also completed a two-month internship program, and subsequently, as a Software Engineer at Gerniks. In both positions, I engaged in diverse projects, including internal initiatives and client-based projects, broadening my skill set and knowledge base. Additionally, during my tenure as a Frontend Developer, I specialized in the Angular framework, contributing to the development of two projects.
